Poetries

Expression

Sometimes
Words aren't enough
To express
The feelings
Beyond the stars
The stories
You told me
Would stay
Forever
And ever
In my heart
The horrors
I can't imagine
But the happiness
You shared
Was real
I hope we stay
Like this
For days to come
And decades to go

At last
Congratulations
For everything you've
Accomplished so far
Why is it so hard
To end this story
When I have so much to say
About your glory!
I can write a novel
On your life
Maybe two
If you told me to write
It's taking time
But its worth
Every second
Every minute
Of my life!

Desire

I play with fire
with a desire
following instructions
of my destruction
a path I chose
took me close
to everything I want
and nothing I truly desire

Repercussions
Cloud my mind
What else is
There to find
I've achieved
What I wanted
But was it
What I desired?
